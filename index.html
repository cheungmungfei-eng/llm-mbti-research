Analyzing LLMs' Understanding of Human MBTI(ENFP & INTJ)

Exploring whether Large Language Models can accurately interpret and respond to questions about personality types

View Analysis

Research Introduction

Research Questions

This study investigates whether LLMs can answer questions about human MBTI. The research addresses three key questions:

  1. Do LLMs understand MBTI?
  2. Could LLMs think about personalities from a human perspective?
  3. How accurate are the LLMs' responses about MBTI?

Methodology

The study devised 17 MBTI-related questions presented to large language models (LLMs) pretending to be people with ENFP or INTJ personality types. Responses were compiled and analyzed using Python for visualization.

Data collected from: Yi-1.5, Qwen2.5, Gemma-3
